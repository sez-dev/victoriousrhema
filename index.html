About Us

At Victorious Rhema Family Foundation, we are driven by a passion to make a meaningful difference in the lives of orphans, less privileged, widows, physically disabled and vulnerable children. We are thrilled to share our recent activities, achievements and future plans with you. We wish you a nice time ahead as we share our story.
